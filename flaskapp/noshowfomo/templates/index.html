DataYetti

A website about data, music and sleep science.

My name is Ben Yetton. I'm curious kiwi with a passion for all things data science, especially probabilistic modeling and deep learning. Check out some of my projects below.

No Mo' Show FOMO: An app to predict if an upcoming concert will sell out.

MODA Sleep Spindles: The highest validity dataset of sleep spindles from human EEG, now available to the public.

The Tesseract: A spinning, flashing, cube shaped DJ lighting system.

Yetton REM Detector: the best Rapid Eye Movement feature detector available. Download for Matlab available.

Pysleep [WIP]: A python library for analyzing sleep. A work in progress.

Publication List: 5 first author publications and counting.
